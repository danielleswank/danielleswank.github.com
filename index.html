    about

    I am a 11-year web development, graphic design, and information architecture veteran. I am a founder at Barking Mouse Studio and I used to be the UX Director at ARS Interactive. I currently make iPhone and iPad games for a living.
    My current game was featured by Apple and won a bunch of awards and stuff. In the past, In my spare time I've built a visual redis administrator, reddi.sh, a social news reader, love.freeflow.io and Bumble Bounce.
    BTW: I also know C#, Coffeescript, Photoshop and Illustrator and have a background in art and print design. I also used to be a volunteer fire fighter and renegade bronze caster (but those are different stories).
