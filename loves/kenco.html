So what is this project?

Kenco is one of the largest family-owned and privately-held third-party logistics providers in the country. They are a very innovative company and created their first website in the 90's. The problem was that their website had been added to and added to over the years without a real plan. They reached out to my company to see what we could do. I led a full site restructuring from an IA perspective, created user flows and personas and then built the whole thing with the help of my amazing team of crack engineers and designers.

Ok cool, so what did you actually do?

  • Creative direction and brand style guide development.
  • User personas. I did hours of interviews with stakeholders of different divisions to come up with several ideal users and tailored the site to them.
  • Sorted hundreds of IA cards. Seriously, we took over a conference room table for nearly a week.
  • Project management. My boss let me run with the project (thanks Jay). I did everything from creating a deck, to giving the initial pitch, to follow up meetings, to creative direction, to building the site with the help of my team.
